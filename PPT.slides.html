Brief overview of the key analysis/argument of the paper.¶

-1.region of origin does not significantly affect earnings.
-2.gender is a more significant factor, men in general earn significantly more than women in earnings among Arab immigrants even if women have equal or even higher educational levels than men.
-3.region of origin is important in terms of gender.
In specific, male immigrants from North Africa earn significantly less than those from the Levant.
